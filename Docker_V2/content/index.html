Welcome to K8s Land

K8s Land is a website that showcases the power and flexibility of Kubernetes, the open-source system for automating deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications1

On this website, you will learn about the features and benefits of Kubernetes, how to get started with it, and how to use it for your own projects. You will also find resources, tutorials, and examples that will help you master Kubernetes and become a cloud-native expert.

Kubernetes is designed on the same principles that allow Google to run billions of containers a week, and can scale without increasing your operations team1 Whether testing locally or running a global enterprise, Kubernetes flexibility grows with you to deliver your applications consistently and easily no matter how complex your need is1

Kubernetes works with various container runtimes, such as containerd and CRI-O, and supports multiple platforms, such as Windows, Linux, and macOS2 You can run Kubernetes on-premises, hybrid, or public cloud infrastructure, letting you effortlessly move workloads to where it matters to you1

Kubernetes defines a set of building blocks (“primitives”) that collectively provide mechanisms that deploy, maintain, and scale applications based on CPU, memory or custom metrics2 Kubernetes also provides tools and services for service discovery, load balancing, networking, storage, security, monitoring, logging, and more2

Kubernetes is open source giving you the freedom to customize it according to your needs. You can also contribute to the development and improvement of Kubernetes by joining the community of thousands of developers and users who are passionate about cloud-native technologies1
